Our Story

4 Years of Creative Excellence

Founded by visionary educator Nunna Pawan Kumar, Legendary Animation began with a clear mission — to provide world-class animation and VFX training in Kakinada, making quality creative education accessible to aspiring artists across coastal Andhra Pradesh.

Today, we're proud to be the best and largest training institute in the region, offering 6 professional courses with 100% practical curriculum and dedicated placement assistance.

Nunna Pawan Kumar
"My passion is to transform talented individuals into skilled professionals who create magic on screen. Every student's success is my greatest achievement."

Nunna Pawan Kumar is a visionary creative educator with deep expertise in Animation, VFX, and Film Production. With a burning passion for the creative arts and a mission to democratize high-quality animation training in Kakinada, he founded Legendary Animation to fill a crucial gap in creative education in coastal Andhra Pradesh.

Under his guidance, Legendary Animation has grown from a small studio to the largest and most respected animation training institute in the region, having successfully trained over 500+ students who now work in leading studios across India.
